BARCELONA

In 1899, Hans Gamper gathered a group of ex-pats from Switzerland living in Barcelona and formed FC Barcelona. Gamper wanted a club that was inclusive, that reflected the intercultural city, and was democratic. From these founding values came the club’s motto: “Mas Que Un Club” (More Than A Club). Their home jersey colors blue and red inspired the nickname “Blaugrana.” The Catalan flag is incorporated into their badge on all Barcelona jerseys.
